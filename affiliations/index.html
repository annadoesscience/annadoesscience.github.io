Scientific organizations

Current and past affiliations.

Polish Society of Human and Evolution Sciences 🇵🇱 Function: Executive Board and social media management
PTNCE connects scientists and students interested in applying evolutionary perspectives to biology, human behavior, and cultural studies.
Fun fact: Only ≈70% of PTNCE members are Polish and the conferences are organized all around Europe!

Centre for Language Evolution Studies 🇵🇱
CLES at the Faculty of Languages, Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń, investigates various aspects of the science of language evolution. See here for the updated list of ongoing projects.

Interdepartmental Student Association ‘Goryl’ 🇵🇱❤️
‘Goryl’ (eng. gorilla) is a student association at the Department of Human Biology, University of Wrocław, Poland. Goryl associates students fascinated with the biological basis of human behaviour.

European Human Behaviour and Evolution Association 🇪🇺
EHBEA is a cross-disciplinary organization dedicated to fostering the work of European scholars exploring evolutionary explanations for human cognition, behaviour, and society.

Evolutionary Psychology Lab 🇺🇸
EPL studies psychological aspects of close relationships, including jealousy, infidelity, and relationship satisfaction.

Battistuzzi’s Bioinformatics Lab 🇺🇸
The lab utilizes custom-made computer programs to better analyze large biological data sets. The main goal of the lab is to improve the understanding of ancient prokaryote evolution and to investigate the evolutionary origin of infectious diseases.

Human Behavior & Evolution Society 🇺🇸
HBES is a society for those studying the evolution of human behaviour. Scientific perspectives range from evolutionary psychology to evolutionary anthropology and cultural evolution.

HumeLaboratory 🇫🇮
Evolutionary-oriented lab at the Åbo Akademi University in Finland. The lab’s research is focused on genomic conflict and kinship, evolutionary psychological medicine, and social cognition.
